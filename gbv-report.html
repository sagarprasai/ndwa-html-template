GBV Facts in Nepal

  • 1 in 3 women in Nepal experience gender-based violence
  • Women with disabilities face 2-3 times higher risk of violence
  • Only 15% of GBV cases are reported to authorities
  • Early reporting can help prevent further violence and connect you with support services
