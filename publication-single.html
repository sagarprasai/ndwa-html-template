NDWA Advocacy Toolkit

A comprehensive guide designed for women and organizations working to advocate for rights, accessibility, and inclusion of women and girls with disabilities in Nepal. This toolkit provides practical strategies, tools, and resources for effective advocacy campaigns.

Published June 2025
Format PDF
Pages 45
Language English

This comprehensive toolkit is designed for women with disabilities, their organizations, civil society organizations, government agencies, educators, and policy makers working to advocate for rights, accessibility, and inclusion of women and girls with disabilities in Nepal. The toolkit provides evidence-based strategies grounded in NDWA's extensive experience, featuring practical examples and case studies from Nepal, downloadable templates and tools, and an accessible design for users with disabilities.

The toolkit covers essential topics including understanding disability rights advocacy, developing advocacy strategies, building coalitions and partnerships, media and communication strategies, policy advocacy and engagement, monitoring and evaluation, along with case studies, success stories, and ready-to-use templates and checklists. Available in both English and Nepali, this resource serves as a comprehensive guide for effective advocacy campaigns and rights-based interventions.
