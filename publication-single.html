NDWA Advocacy Toolkit

Published: June 2025
Format: PDF
Pages: 45
Language: English
Downloads: 1,240

Overview

A comprehensive guide designed for women and organizations working to advocate for rights, accessibility, and inclusion of women and girls with disabilities in Nepal. This toolkit provides practical strategies, tools, and resources for effective advocacy campaigns.

Contents

  • Understanding disability rights advocacy
  • Developing advocacy strategies
  • Building coalitions and partnerships
  • Media and communication strategies
  • Policy advocacy and engagement
  • Monitoring and evaluation
  • Case studies and success stories
  • Templates and checklists

Who Should Use This?

This toolkit is designed for:

  • Women with disabilities and their organizations
  • Civil society organizations working on disability rights
  • Government and development agencies
  • Educators and trainers
  • Policy makers and advocates

Key Features

  • Evidence-based strategies grounded in NDWA's experience
  • Practical examples and case studies from Nepal
  • Downloadable templates and tools
  • Accessible design for users with disabilities
  • Available in English and Nepali
